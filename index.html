MY BACKGROUND

Growing up in Mumbai, the bustling commercial capital of India, my home was a fusion of creativity and analytical thinking. This unique blend has deeply shaped my perspective.

My fascination with technology began early, sparked by our trusty beige computer. my true passion lies in the transformative power of great design craft.

My curiosity led me to wonder: could I also create something that can evoke this effect on people?

Today, I apply my multidisciplinary design skills, aiming to create solutions that make people feel and remember, elevating everyday experiences into inspiring solutions.
