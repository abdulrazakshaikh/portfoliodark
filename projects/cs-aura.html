Overview

Aura Fitness
Health & Wellness App

Aura Fitness aims to revolutionize how individuals track their daily workouts, connect with fitness communities, and achieve their health goals.

The objective was to design a highly engaging, gamified mobile application that motivates users to stay active while offering precise tracking analytics.

High Level Goals

01

Create an intuitive, low-friction onboarding flow to quickly capture user health data.

02

Design a gamified dashboard with motivational cues to increase daily active usage.

Process

Initial Challenges

Many fitness apps suffer from high drop-off rates after the first week. Users found manual tracking tedious and lacked the motivation to return.

Research & Immersion

I conducted ethnographic research with gym-goers and fitness enthusiasts. We mapped out their pain points, prioritizing one-tap logging and visual progress indicators.

Trust Building Strategy

We built interactive, high-fidelity prototypes demonstrating micro-animations for workout completions. This helped stakeholders visualize the 'feel-good' moments of the app.

Crafting the Style Mood Board

Recognizing the brand's exacting standards, I designed a style mood board to effectively communicate my design process to the Team.

Style-tiles mood boards are very effective in showing where a design came from

First Concept

The initial concept utilized a dark mode aesthetic with vibrant neon accents to simulate the high-energy environment of a premium fitness studio.

Aura Fitness Solution

Aura Fitness evolved into a comprehensive digital companion for health enthusiasts.

Seamless Activity Tracking

Integrated with device health kits for automated, zero-friction workout logging and step counting.

Community Leaderboards

Added social features that allow users to compete with friends, drastically improving retention rates.

Impact

The application successfully captured a highly engaged user base with impressive retention metrics.

300% Increase in DAU
4.8★ App Store Rating

Habit Formation

Over 60% of registered users actively log workouts at least 3 times a week.

Key Learnings

My journey through this project provided several key insights.

Gaining Trust with High-Fidelity

While wireframes are valuable, in this case, a high-fidelity prototype proved indispensable in gaining the client's trust. This trust, in turn, allowed for deeper discovery work.

Pick Your Battles Wisely

Knowing when to make trade-offs and when to prioritize critical elements is essential, especially when collaborating with external clients and your tech team.

Be Prepared to Adapt

User testing can present surprising challenges, emphasizing the need for adaptability and quick adjustments when an unexpected situation happen.
