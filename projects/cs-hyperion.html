Overview

HyperionBCC
Agency Experience

When HyperionBCC needed to establish a strong digital presence, they sought an experience that reflected their strategic consulting expertise. The challenge was to create a modern, corporate yet engaging agency website.

We aimed to restructure their complex navigation and deliver a seamless mobile-first approach, communicating authority and clarity in every interaction.

High Level Goals

01

Simplify complex service structures into a clear top-level mega menu navigation.

02

Establish a strong, authoritative visual language that builds trust with enterprise clients.

Process

Initial Challenges

The main challenge was a 78% bounce rate caused by a confusing 3-level deep dropdown navigation and broken mobile views. Visitors couldn't quickly find what they were looking for.

Research & Immersion

I conducted a systematic heuristic evaluation against 10 usability heuristics, documenting over 30 specific issues. We mapped out card sorting exercises with real users to re-categorize their consulting services.

Trust Building Strategy

To secure stakeholder buy-in, I created interactive, high-fidelity prototypes demonstrating the new mobile-first mega-menu. This tangible artifact aligned the consulting partners around a single vision.

Crafting the Style Mood Board

Recognizing the brand's exacting standards, I designed a style mood board to effectively communicate my design process to the Team.

Style-tiles mood boards are very effective in showing where a design came from

First Concept

The first concept introduced a clean, white-space heavy layout with a customized serif typography stack. I prioritized touch-friendly targets and full-screen hamburger menus for the mobile view.

HyperionBCC Solution

The delivered solution is a fast, 95+ PageSpeed scoring website that positions HyperionBCC as a premier consultancy.

Mobile-First Accessibility

By rebuilding the mobile layout from the ground up, we ensured that the site is entirely navigable on any device.

Elevated Brand Authority

We swapped generic layouts for custom iconography and strong typographical hierarchy, reducing cognitive load.

Impact

The new design drastically improved user retention and engagement on mobile devices.

55% Bounce Rate Reduction
40% Mobile Session Increase

Performance Focused

Achieved a 95+ Google PageSpeed score across both mobile and desktop platforms.

Key Learnings

My journey through this project provided several key insights.

Gaining Trust with High-Fidelity

While wireframes are valuable, in this case, a high-fidelity prototype proved indispensable in gaining the client's trust. This trust, in turn, allowed for deeper discovery work.

Pick Your Battles Wisely

Knowing when to make trade-offs and when to prioritize critical elements is essential, especially when collaborating with external clients and your tech team.

Be Prepared to Adapt

User testing can present surprising challenges, emphasizing the need for adaptability and quick adjustments when an unexpected situation happen.
