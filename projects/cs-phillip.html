Overview

Phillip1
Investment Platform

PhillipCapital needed to consolidate their fragmented asset management tools into a single, unified back-office application: Phillip1.

Our focus was on designing an integrated platform that simplifies complex financial data, allowing users to effortlessly monitor their varied asset classes without feeling overwhelmed.

High Level Goals

01

Create an integrated dashboard that consolidates all asset classes into one unified view.

02

Ensure data security and transparency while maintaining a seamless user experience.

Process

Initial Challenges

Traders and investors were using multiple disjointed applications, leading to a fragmented user experience. The challenge was merging different data streams without cluttering the UI.

Research & Immersion

I interviewed financial advisors and active traders to understand their daily workflows. We prioritized critical data points like portfolio performance, real-time market updates, and transaction history.

Trust Building Strategy

We prototyped a unified dashboard featuring customizable widgets. This allowed stakeholders to physically interact with the data visualization concepts before we committed to development.

Crafting the Style Mood Board

Recognizing the brand's exacting standards, I designed a style mood board to effectively communicate my design process to the Team.

Style-tiles mood boards are very effective in showing where a design came from

First Concept

The initial concept centered around a dark-mode optimized interface tailored for long trading hours. It featured bold, color-coded typography to immediately signal market trends (green for up, red for down).

Phillip1 Solution

Phillip1 emerged as a comprehensive, secure app that serves as the central hub for all investment activities.

Unified Portfolio View

Users can now see their stocks, mutual funds, and IPO investments on a single, intuitive screen.

Seamless Transitions

We implemented smooth micro-interactions that make switching between different asset categories feel instantaneous and native.

Impact

The application streamlined back-office operations and significantly enhanced client satisfaction.

4.5★ Play Store Rating
3x Faster Data Retrieval

High Adoption Rate

Quickly became the preferred platform for active clients due to its reliability and clean design.

Key Learnings

My journey through this project provided several key insights.

Gaining Trust with High-Fidelity

While wireframes are valuable, in this case, a high-fidelity prototype proved indispensable in gaining the client's trust. This trust, in turn, allowed for deeper discovery work.

Pick Your Battles Wisely

Knowing when to make trade-offs and when to prioritize critical elements is essential, especially when collaborating with external clients and your tech team.

Be Prepared to Adapt

User testing can present surprising challenges, emphasizing the need for adaptability and quick adjustments when an unexpected situation happen.
