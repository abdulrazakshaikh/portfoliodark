Overview

Greenscape
Real Estate Platform

Greenscape Group, premier real estate builders and developers in Navi Mumbai, needed a digital showcase that matched the elegance and quality of their physical properties.

We focused on immersive imagery, smooth transitions, and a streamlined property inquiry process to turn digital visitors into physical leads.

Company Greenscape Group

High Level Goals

01

Showcase high-end properties with immersive, high-resolution media without sacrificing performance.

02

Optimize the user journey to encourage inquiries and site visits.

Process

Initial Challenges

The old website felt dated and failed to convey the premium nature of the properties. Load times were slow due to unoptimized images.

Research & Immersion

We conducted a visual audit and decided to prioritize large-scale lifestyle photography and 3D walkthroughs, ensuring the user feels the space virtually.

Trust Building Strategy

We presented a minimalist, luxury-focused design language to the stakeholders, emphasizing typography and generous white space to let the properties shine.

Crafting the Style Mood Board

Recognizing the brand's exacting standards, I designed a style mood board to effectively communicate my design process to the Team.

Style-tiles mood boards are very effective in showing where a design came from

First Concept

The first concept introduced a cinematic scroll experience, where properties faded in dynamically as the user scrolled down, mimicking a guided tour.

Greenscape Solution

The final product is a digital brochure that breathes luxury and drives conversions.

Immersive Galleries

Implemented highly optimized, responsive image grids and virtual tour integrations.

Frictionless Contact

Strategically placed sticky contact forms and WhatsApp integrations ensure leads are captured instantly.

Impact

The redesign transformed their online presence into a powerful lead generation engine.

3x Increase in Leads
60% Longer Session Duration

Brand Elevation

The modern aesthetic firmly positioned Greenscape as a top-tier developer in the Navi Mumbai region.

Key Learnings

My journey through this project provided several key insights.

Gaining Trust with High-Fidelity

While wireframes are valuable, in this case, a high-fidelity prototype proved indispensable in gaining the client's trust. This trust, in turn, allowed for deeper discovery work.

Pick Your Battles Wisely

Knowing when to make trade-offs and when to prioritize critical elements is essential, especially when collaborating with external clients and your tech team.

Be Prepared to Adapt

User testing can present surprising challenges, emphasizing the need for adaptability and quick adjustments when an unexpected situation happen.
