Overview

Adani Logistics
IFF Portal

The Adani IFF (Integrated Freight Forwarding) platform required a comprehensive overhaul to manage immense logistical data at scale.

The project involved designing a robust, enterprise-grade portal capable of tracking shipments, managing documents, and streamlining communication across global ports.

Company Adani Group
My Role Lead Designer
Timeline 6 Months
Responsibilities Design Direction

High Level Goals

01

Design an enterprise dashboard capable of handling thousands of active shipments seamlessly.

02

Ensure strict compliance and data security while providing real-time visibility.

Process

Initial Challenges

Operators were struggling with legacy systems that crashed under the weight of real-time tracking data, causing costly delays.

Research & Immersion

I engaged in deep ethnographic research, observing freight forwarders and customs officers. We mapped out the critical path for a shipment from origin to destination.

Trust Building Strategy

We utilized a design system tailored for high-density data, creating prototypes that demonstrated how complex tables and tracking logs could be filtered and sorted instantly.

Crafting the Style Mood Board

Recognizing the brand's exacting standards, I designed a style mood board to effectively communicate my design process to the Team.

Style-tiles mood boards are very effective in showing where a design came from

First Concept

The first concept introduced a split-view interface, allowing operators to view a shipment map on one side while managing customs documentation on the other.

Adani Logistics Solution

The IFF portal transformed a chaotic process into a streamlined, predictable workflow.

Real-Time Tracking

Integrated live API feeds for vessel tracking, providing operators with up-to-the-minute status updates.

Document Hub

A centralized, secure repository for all customs and freight documentation, eliminating email chains.

Impact

The system dramatically increased the operational throughput of the logistics division.

30% Efficiency Gain
10k+ Shipments/Month

Enterprise Scale

Successfully deployed across multiple regional offices, standardizing operations globally.

Key Learnings

My journey through this project provided several key insights.

Gaining Trust with High-Fidelity

While wireframes are valuable, in this case, a high-fidelity prototype proved indispensable in gaining the client's trust. This trust, in turn, allowed for deeper discovery work.

Pick Your Battles Wisely

Knowing when to make trade-offs and when to prioritize critical elements is essential, especially when collaborating with external clients and your tech team.

Be Prepared to Adapt

User testing can present surprising challenges, emphasizing the need for adaptability and quick adjustments when an unexpected situation happen.
